Santa Barbara Go Club

The Santa Barbara Go Club meets every Saturday at Dean's Coffee Shop (6100 Hollister Ave, Goleta, CA) 11:00 am to 3 pm

We also play online Wednesday nights in the Santa Barbara Go Club room on the KGS go server.

Beginners and all levels welcome. It's free. Enjoy learning and playing this ancient and beautiful game!

For any further information, contact Lorin at lorinlevin@yahoo.com
